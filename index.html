Plethora of Events Unfold

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering - Artificial Intelligence (CSE-AI) organized an insightful event on October 1, 2024, at SBG Hall, focusing on the applications of Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity in today’s world.


The event featured distinguished industry experts and professionals, including Mr. Willem Bos (Senior Director, Professional Manager, Global Scale Team, Palo Alto Networks), Mr. Lalit Sanwal (Senior Service Delivery Manager, Global Scale Team, Palo Alto Networks), and Mr. Rakesh Goyal(MD & CEO, Infinity Labs Ltd.). They shared valuable insights on the growing role of AI and Cybersecurity, emphasizing their importance in modern industries and the need for skilled professionals.


The discussion highlighted how AI-driven innovations enhance security measures and the significance of preparing young graduates with technical expertise. Attendees had the opportunity to engage with industry leaders, gaining deeper perspectives on the evolving technological landscape.

VIVIDHATA After the event concluded, an engaging Q&A session allowed attendees to interact with the esteemed speakers, gaining deeper insights into the industry. Many students were particularly interested in career opportunities and pathways to enter leading organizations like Palo Alto Networks. The speakers shared their experiences, emphasizing the importance of strong fundamentals in networking, security protocols, and hands-on experience with cybersecurity tools. They encouraged students to pursue relevant certifications such as Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), CISSP, and Palo Alto Networks certifications to improve their prospects in the field.

Another key area of discussion revolved around breaking into the cybersecurity domain. The speakers advised aspiring professionals to start with basic cybersecurity concepts, participate in capture-the-flag (CTF) competitions, and gain practical exposure through internships and personal projects. They highlighted the growing demand for ethical hackers and security analysts in today’s digital world and stressed the importance of continuous learning to stay ahead of emerging threats.

Students also inquired about the topics they should focus on to enhance their industry exposure in AI and cybersecurity. The experts suggested areas such as AI-driven threat detection, cloud security, zero-trust architectures, and secure software development. They also emphasized the role of machine learning in cybersecurity and recommended hands-on projects that integrate AI techniques with security frameworks.

The discussion then shifted to consultancy companies and how they operate in the technology sector. The speakers explained that consultancy firms provide strategic advice, risk assessments, and tailored cybersecurity solutions to businesses. They described how consultants analyze an organization’s security posture, implement AI-driven automation, and help companies mitigate risks. The session provided students with a clear understanding of the skills required to work in consultancy roles.

Lastly, a specific query was raised regarding Infinity Labs and its operations. Mr. Rakesh Goyal elaborated on how Infinity Labs specializes in providing innovative technology solutions, including AI-powered automation, cloud security, and enterprise networking services. He discussed the company’s approach to problem-solving and innovation, encouraging students to explore internship opportunities at Infinity Labs or similar ventures to gain hands-on industry experience.

Overall, the interactive session provided students with valuable guidance on career paths, industry trends, and skill development, equipping them with the knowledge needed to excel in AI and cybersecurity.

Is this the Real Life or is this Fantasy

During the AR-VR IN ACTION workshop, experts highlighted the practical application of augmented reality (AR) through tools like Snapchat's filters, known as "Lenses." These Lenses allow users to overlay digital effects onto their real-world environment in real-time, transforming their appearance or surroundings. Snapchat's Lens Studio is a powerful desktop application that enables artists and developers to create these interactive AR experiences. With Lens Studio, users can design custom Lenses, incorporating 3D objects, animations, and interactive elements, which can then be shared with Snapchat's vast user base. This platform has democratized AR development, allowing both novices and professionals to contribute to the growing library of immersive content on Snapchat. ​


Team Red versus Team Blue

The Technicalevent Vividhata Club marked another milestone by hosting Capture the Flag, an engaging session on the fundamentals of cybersecurity. Designed for aspiring problem-solvers and ethical hackers, the event introduced participants to Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges, a key component of global cybersecurity competitions. The session covered different CTF formats, including Jeopardy-style challenges (cryptography, web security, reverse engineering) and Attack-Defense CTFs, where teams secure their systems while exploiting opponents' vulnerabilities. Hands-on exercises provided real-world cybersecurity exposure, helping attendees develop problem-solving and hacking skills. Beyond technical learning, the event sparked a passion for cybersecurity, with many participants eager to explore advanced topics like penetration testing and ethical hacking. Encouraged by the enthusiasm, the club aims to organize advanced workshops, team-based CTFs, and mentorship programs, ensuring continued engagement and skill development in cybersecurity.


The Bharat Shiksha Expo Experience


The Vividhata Club proudly participated in Bharat Shiksha Expo 2024 , leaving a lasting impact by seamlessly blending creativity and technology through a diverse range of engaging activities. The event served as a dynamic platform to showcase innovation, teamwork, and artistic expression, allowing participants to explore and push the boundaries of their skills. Over the course of three action-packed days, the club hosted an exciting lineup of interactive events, each designed to foster learning, collaboration, and fun.

With an overwhelming response from students, faculty members, and visitors, the event attracted over 500 participants, each actively engaging with the club’s initiatives. The diverse activities catered to different interests, ensuring an inclusive experience for all attendees. The interactive, hands-on nature of these events provided a valuable learning experience, allowing students to step beyond traditional academic settings and explore interdisciplinary fields. From technology and design to problem-solving and teamwork, the event highlighted the importance of cross-domain interaction and creative exploration.


Beyond just entertainment, the experience left a profound impact on students and visitors, inspiring them to embrace innovation, take on new challenges, and step out of their comfort zones. The event not only enhanced their skills but also nurtured their confidence in expressing ideas and engaging in collaborative efforts.


Encouraged by the success of Bharat Shiksha Expo 2024, the Vividhata Club is committed to expanding its outreach, introducing more engaging workshops, competitions, and collaborative initiatives in the future. By continuously promoting creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork, the club aims to strengthen its role as a dynamic and influential part of the academic community, inspiring students to explore, innovate, and thrive in their respective fields.


Think Before you Click

GL Bajaj Institute of Technology & Management hosted a Safer Internet Day workshop on February 11, 2025, in collaboration with C-DAC Noida under MeitY’s ISEA Project. The event aimed to promote cyber awareness and responsible digital practices among students and faculty members.


Guest lecturer Kajal Kashyap led an engaging and informative session, covering key aspects of cyber hygiene, online threats, and responsible internet usage. She emphasized the importance of strong password management, phishing awareness, safe browsing habits, and privacy protection in an increasingly digital world. Through real-life case studies and interactive discussions, attendees gained valuable insights into recognizing and mitigating potential cybersecurity risks.


The workshop served as a crucial learning opportunity, equipping participants with practical strategies to enhance their online security. By fostering awareness and proactive digital habits, the session empowered attendees to navigate the internet safely and responsibly, reinforcing the importance of cybersecurity in daily life.
